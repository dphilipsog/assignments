The mean amount spent on commercial transportation by the House of Representatives during the first quarter of 2017 was around 2860 USD. Nonetheless, the median amount was around 222 USD. Thus, the variante of amounts spent on commercial transportation is quite high. 

Out of those who spent above the median amount on comercial transportation, the Chief Officer of the House reported the most bills for expensive comercial transportation (33), followed by the Office of Hon. David G. Valadao (32), and the Office of Hon. Stevan Pearce (31).

The most expensive commercial transportation bill was spent by an anonymous “new member of orientation”. However, the next most expensive bill for the same purpose was submitted by Hon. Madeleine Z. Bordallo.
